# Product: MENToBe

MENToBe is a Tinder-style mentorship matching platform that connects students and early-career graduates (mentees, ages 18–28) with experienced professionals (mentors, ages 30–55). It is a digital-only product delivered as a mobile app (iOS 14+ / Android 10+) with a companion web experience.

## Core problem

76% of professionals say mentors matter; only 37% have one. LinkedIn cold outreach lands 2–5% reply rates. Mentees lack structured access; mentors lack efficient triage.

## Core value proposition

Dating-app efficiency applied to career-specific matching: skills, teaching styles, availability, language, and values are all weighted by an AI matching algorithm.

## Primary user roles

- **Mentee** — seeks guidance, swipes on mentors, manages limited active connections (3 on free tier).
- **Mentor** — offers expertise, swipes on mentee requests, may run group workspaces with multiple mentees.

## MVP feature pillars

1. **Auth & role selection** — Google/email login, then choose mentee or mentor.
2. **Profile setup** — multi-step onboarding capturing skills, interests, teaching style, experience, availability, languages, values. Mentors include a verification step.
3. **AI matching & swipe** — curated cards with match-percentage badges; right swipe = interest, left swipe = pass. Mutual right swipe creates a match.
4. **Messaging** — unlocked on match, with AI conversation prompts to seed the first exchange.
5. **AI meeting notes** — discussion summary, action items, next-meeting goals, shared resources.
6. **Profile & calendar** — availability windows, calendar sync, plan/preference management.

## Business model

Freemium with tiers: Free (3 active connections), Mentee Premium ($9.99/mo), Mentor Pro ($4.99/mo or free for verified mentors), Enterprise/University ($199–$999/mo).

## Success metrics

- 60%+ of matches lead to ≥3 meetings within 90 days
- 40%+ MAU retention at 6 months
- 2.5 active mentee connections per mentor on average
- 15%+ premium conversion within 6 months
- 35%+ of mentees report tangible career progress within 12 months
